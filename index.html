Research interests

During my PhD, I mainly studied applications of derived algebraic geometry and algebraic topology in the context of the geometric Langlands program, and in particular to the role of the affine Grassmannian and its variants. Recently, I have also become interested in the study of algebraic K-theory and real hermitian K-theory from an $\infty$-categorical viewpoint.

Keywords: Algebraic geometry, Algebraic topology, Category theory, Derived algebraic geometry, Geometric representation theory, $\infty$-category theory.
